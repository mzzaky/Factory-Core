  OVERVIEW
  --------
  FactoryCore is a factory-based economy plugin for Minecraft Spigot/Paper
  servers. It creates a manufacturing-driven economy where players collect
  raw resources, process them through various factory types, and sell
  finished products. The Machine Parts system creates a constant demand
  cycle that encourages specialization and trade between players.

  REQUIRED DEPENDENCIES: Vault, WorldEdit, WorldGuard
  OPTIONAL DEPENDENCIES: PlaceholderAPI

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  [1] FACTORY SYSTEM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  + 4 Factory Types:
    - Steel Mill     : Converts raw resources into advanced resources
                       Base Price: $100,000 | Icon: IRON_BLOCK
    - Refinery       : Produces fuel for all factories
                       Base Price: $150,000 | Icon: FURNACE
    - Workshop       : Produces machine parts consumed by ALL factories
                       Base Price: $200,000 | Icon: ANVIL
    - Advanced Factory: Produces high-value final products for sale
                       Base Price: $250,000 | Icon: NETHER_STAR

  + Factory Ownership System
    - Players can buy and own factories
    - Factory sell price: 50% of purchase price (configurable)
    - Factories are bound to WorldGuard regions

  + Factory Level System (1-5 levels):
    - Production cost reduction: 5% per level
    - Production time reduction: 10% per level
    - Additional storage slots: +9 per level
    - Additional tax: +2.5% per level
    - Upgrade cost scales: Level 2 = 50% of factory price, etc.

  + Factory Status Tracking:
    - RUNNING, STOPPED, OUT OF MACHINE PARTS

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  [2] PRODUCTION SYSTEM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  + 16 Predefined Recipes across all factory types:

    Steel Mill Recipes:
    - Steel Ingot Production     (300s) : 5 Iron + 2 Coal + 1 Fuel + 1 Gear -> 10 Steel
    - Carbon Fiber Production    (450s) : 10 Coal + 3 Iron + 2 Refined Fuel + 1 Adv Gear -> 8 Carbon Fiber

    Refinery Recipes:
    - Basic Fuel Production      (200s) : 8 Coal + 1 Basic Gear -> 16 Basic Fuel
    - Refined Fuel Production    (350s) : 12 Coal + 4 Iron + 1 Adv Gear -> 10 Refined Fuel
    - Premium Fuel Production    (500s) : 20 Coal + 5 Gold + 1 Circuit Board -> 8 Premium Fuel

    Workshop Recipes:
    - Basic Gear Production      (250s) : 8 Iron + 4 Redstone + 2 Fuel + 1 Gear -> 6 Basic Gear
    - Advanced Gear Production   (400s) : 6 Gold + 8 Redstone + 3 Ref Fuel + 1 Adv Gear -> 5 Adv Gear
    - Engine Part Production     (350s) : 10 Iron + 5 Copper + 6 Redstone + 3 Fuel + 1 Engine -> 4 Engine Part
    - Circuit Board Production   (450s) : 12 Copper + 4 Gold + 10 Redstone + 4 Ref Fuel + 1 CB -> 3 Circuit Board
    - Hydraulic System Production(600s) : 15 Iron + 2 Diamond + 12 Redstone + 3 Prem Fuel + 1 HS -> 2 Hydraulic Sys

    Advanced Factory Recipes:
    - Advanced Weapon            (600s) : Steel + Carbon + Copper + Prem Fuel + CB -> 1 Adv Weapon
    - Advanced Armor             (650s) : Steel + Carbon + Gold Alloy + Prem Fuel + HS -> 1 Adv Armor
    - Electronic Component       (500s) : Ref Copper + Gold Alloy + Carbon + Ref Fuel + CB -> 2 Electronic Comp
    - Luxury Item                (800s) : Gold Alloy + Steel + Carbon + Copper + Prem Fuel + HS + CB -> 1 Luxury
    - Refined Copper             (300s) : 10 Copper + 2 Basic Fuel + 1 Engine Part -> 8 Refined Copper
    - Gold Alloy                 (400s) : 8 Gold + 4 Iron + 3 Refined Fuel + 2 Adv Gear -> 6 Gold Alloy

  + BossBar progress indicator during production
  + Production queue system (max 5 queue slots)
  + Console command execution on production completion
  + Economy rewards upon production completion

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  [3] RESOURCE SYSTEM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  + 5 Resource Categories with 21 total resources:

    Raw Resources (6):
    - Iron Ore, Coal Ore, Copper Ore, Gold Ore, Diamond Ore, Redstone Dust

    Advanced Resources (4):
    - Steel Ingot, Refined Copper, Gold Alloy, Carbon Fiber

    Final Resources (4) - Sellable Products:
    - Advanced Weapon    ($5,000)

    - Electronic Component ($3,000)
    - Luxury Item        ($10,000)

    Machine Parts (5):
    - Basic Gear, Advanced Gear, Engine Part, Circuit Board, Hydraulic System

  + Custom Model Data support for all resources
  + Enchantment glow effect for rare items
  + Full item customization via resources.yml

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  [4] ECONOMY & TAX SYSTEM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  + Full Vault integration for all economic transactions
  + Configurable currency symbol and decimal places

  + Tax System:
    - Tax rate: 5% of factory price (configurable)
    - Tax interval: Every 3 Minecraft days (144,000 ticks)
    - Level multiplier: +2.5% additional tax per level
    - Due period: 7 days before overdue
    - Late fee: 5% penalty on overdue taxes
    - Player notifications on tax assessment and overdue

  + Salary System:
    - Rate: 1% of factory price (configurable)
    - Interval: Every 1 Minecraft day (24,000 ticks)

  + Invoice Management:
    - Automatic invoice generation for tax and salary
    - Pay individual or all invoices at once
    - Overdue tracking with penalties
    - Factory disabled on unpaid overdue invoices

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  [5] NPC EMPLOYEE SYSTEM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  + Custom Villager NPCs as factory employees
  + 5 Employee Tiers available in Employee Shop:
    - Apprentice Worker   ($500,   salary $50)   : -5% production time
    - Skilled Craftsman   ($1,500, salary $120)  : -15% production time
    - Steel Forger        ($2,500, salary $200)  : -20% production time
    - Refinery Operator   ($3,500, salary $280)  : -25% production time
    - Master Engineer     ($6,000, salary $450)  : -35% production time

  + Advanced NPC Behavior:
    - Head tracking: NPCs look at nearby players (6 block radius)
    - Idle animations: Random head movement with smooth interpolation
    - Particle effects: Unique particles per factory type
      (VILLAGER_HAPPY, FLAME, SMOKE, CRIT_MAGIC, ENCHANTMENT_TABLE)
    - Hologram display above NPCs
    - Ambient sounds (ENTITY_VILLAGER_AMBIENT)
    - Interaction sounds (ENTITY_VILLAGER_YES)
    - Body rotation following head direction
    - Invulnerable and AI-disabled
    - Auto-respawn check system

  + 4 NPC Templates matching factory types:
    - Steel Mill Worker (ARMORER, PLAINS)
    - Refinery Operator (TOOLSMITH, DESERT)
    - Workshop Engineer (WEAPONSMITH, SNOW)
    - Advanced Factory Manager (LIBRARIAN, JUNGLE)

  RINGKASAN
  ---------
  FactoryCore adalah plugin ekonomi berbasis pabrik untuk server Minecraft
  Spigot/Paper. Plugin ini menciptakan ekonomi berbasis manufaktur di mana
  pemain mengumpulkan sumber daya mentah, memproses melalui berbagai jenis
  pabrik, dan menjual produk jadi. Sistem Machine Parts menciptakan siklus
  permintaan konstan yang mendorong spesialisasi dan perdagangan antar pemain.

  DEPENDENSI WAJIB: Vault, WorldEdit, WorldGuard
  DEPENDENSI OPSIONAL: PlaceholderAPI

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  [1] SISTEM PABRIK
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  + 4 Jenis Pabrik:
    - Steel Mill      : Mengubah raw resources menjadi advanced resources
                        Harga Dasar: $100.000 | Ikon: IRON_BLOCK
    - Refinery        : Memproduksi bahan bakar untuk semua pabrik
                        Harga Dasar: $150.000 | Ikon: FURNACE
    - Workshop        : Memproduksi machine parts yang dikonsumsi SEMUA pabrik
                        Harga Dasar: $200.000 | Ikon: ANVIL
    - Advanced Factory: Memproduksi produk bernilai tinggi untuk dijual
                        Harga Dasar: $250.000 | Ikon: NETHER_STAR

  + Sistem Kepemilikan Pabrik
    - Pemain dapat membeli dan memiliki pabrik
    - Harga jual pabrik: 50% dari harga beli (dapat dikonfigurasi)
    - Pabrik terikat pada region WorldGuard

  + Sistem Level Pabrik (1-5 level):
    - Pengurangan biaya produksi: 5% per level
    - Pengurangan waktu produksi: 10% per level
    - Tambahan slot storage: +9 per level
    - Pajak tambahan: +2,5% per level
    - Biaya upgrade berskala: Level 2 = 50% harga pabrik, dst.

  + Pelacakan Status Pabrik:
    - RUNNING, STOPPED, OUT OF MACHINE PARTS
