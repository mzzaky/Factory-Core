Overview

Core System

The Employee System is a critical component of the FactoryCore plugin. Players hire NPC employees ‚Äî each representing a real Villager entity in the world ‚Äî to staff their factories. Without an employee, a factory cannot produce anything.

üí° Why Hire Employees?
Factories require an assigned employee to start production. Without one, the factory remains completely idle. Employees also provide a Production Time Reduction buff and grant access to the factory's output storage.

Employees are purchased from the in-game Employee Shop, stored in the player's personal employee pool, and then assigned to individual factories. Each factory can hold exactly one employee at a time.

Key Features

Mechanics
‚ö°

Production Speed Boost

Every employee reduces production time by a fixed percentage. Higher-tier employees provide larger reductions, up to -35%.

üè≠

Production Gate

An employee is the "key" to start a factory. Without one assigned, the factory is completely blocked from producing any items.

üí∞

Periodic Salary

Employees charge a salary every 1 in-game day (24,000 ticks). Unassigned employees still cost salary, so manage wisely.

üì¶

Storage Access

Having an employee assigned allows you to interact with and collect items from the factory's output storage GUI.

üé≠

Living NPC

Employees are real Villager entities with head-tracking, idle animations, particle effects, holograms, and ambient sounds.

üîÑ

Auto-Respawn

The system automatically checks for and respawns missing NPC entities every 200 ticks (~10 seconds).

Step-by-Step: Hiring an Employee

  1. Open the Hub ‚Äî Access the FactoryCore main menu.
  2. Go to Employees Center ‚Äî Click the Employees Center button.
  3. Open Employee Shop ‚Äî Click the gold ingot button (slot 46).
  4. Browse & Confirm ‚Äî Click an employee to open the purchase confirmation screen. Review the cost and click Confirm.
  5. Assign to Factory ‚Äî Go to "My Employees", click the newly purchased employee, then select which factory to assign them to.

Configuration

config.yml ¬∑ npc.yml

Salary Settings ‚Äî config.yml

Controls how and when employee salaries are deducted from the factory owner's balance.

config.yml
1 # Salary settings
2 salary:
3 enabled: true
4 rate: 1.0 # Percentage of factory price charged as base salary
5 interval-ticks: 24000 # 1 in-game day (20 ticks = 1 second)
Key Default Description
salary.enabled true Enables or disables the entire salary system.
salary.rate 1.0 Base salary as a percentage of the factory's price. Each NPC type also has its own fixed salary defined in npc.yml.
salary.interval-ticks 24000 How often salary is charged, in server ticks. 24,000 = 1 in-game day.
