Overview

Factory Types form the core progression system of FactoryCore. Rather than having a single building that does everything, the economy is divided into specialized factory types. Players must build and interlink different types of factories to create complex supply chains.

üí° Progression Concept
Raw materials like Iron Ore and Coal are first processed in a Steel Mill or Refinery. The outputs are then used by a Workshop to create components, which are finally assembled into high-value items in an Advanced Factory.

Steel Mill

The Steel Mill is the foundation of your industrial operations. It takes basic raw resources like Iron Ore and Coal Ore and refines them into sturdy, advanced materials that are necessary for almost all mid-to-late game recipes.

Property Value
Base Price $100,000.0
Icon Identity IRON_BLOCK
Main Purpose Raw Resource Refinement

Key Productions

  • Steel Ingot: Combines Iron Ore, Coal Ore, and Fuel. A must-have for weapons and armor.
  • Carbon Fiber: High-end material demanding a mix of ores and refined fuel.

Refinery

Without power, production halts. The Refinery specializes in synthesizing basic ores into highly efficient fuels. This fuel is a required input for producing advanced components in the Workshop and Advanced Factory.

Property Value
Base Price $150,000.0
Icon Identity FURNACE
Main Purpose Fuel Generation

Key Productions

  • Basic Fuel: Essential to kickstart basic Workshop operations.
  • Refined Fuel & Premium Fuel: Required for top-tier manufacturing and luxury items.

Workshop

The Workshop is where refined materials and fuels come together to create precision components. If players want to build complex machinery, they will need a steady supply of items manufactured here.

Property Value
Base Price $200,000.0
Icon Identity ANVIL
Main Purpose Component Manufacturing

Key Productions

  • Basic & Advanced Gears: Foundational mechanical parts.
  • Engine Parts & Hydraulic Systems: Heavy-duty components used in late-game assembly.
  • Circuit Boards: The brain of any advanced electronic mechanism.

Advanced Factory

The Advanced Factory is the pinnacle of the FactoryCore progression tree. It requires huge capital to purchase but allows players to assemble components from all other factories into incredibly valuable, highly sought-after final products.

Property Value
Base Price $250,000.0
Icon Identity NETHER_STAR
Main Purpose Final Assembly & High-Value Goods

Key Productions

  • Advanced Weapons & Armor: The ultimate combat gear crafted from Steel and Carbon Fiber.
  • Electronic Components: Essential for high-tech setups.
  • Luxury Items: Consume massive amounts of high-end parts yielding the highest economic return.

Developer Tips

As a developer or server administrator, FactoryCore gives you full control over how you define, price, and structure your factory types. Everything is highly flexible and data-driven via configuration files.

Factory Types are strictly enumerated within the Java plugin (FactoryType.java) right now, but their display attributes and pricing are completely managed in config.yml. Alter pricing here to instantly balance your server's economy.
config.yml 
factory-types:
  steel_mill:
    name: "¬ß6Steel Mill"
    base-price: 100000.0
    icon: IRON_BLOCK

When writing your own custom recipes in recipes.yml, the factory-type field must exactly match the internal ID of the factory (e.g., steel_mill, refinery). A recipe with an invalid type will be ignored during loading.
recipes.yml 
recipes:
  custom_plastic:
    name: "¬ßfPlastic Production"
    factory-type: refinery       # Matches the enum / config key
    production-time: 150
    money-cost: 25.0
    inputs:
      coal: 5
    outputs:
      plastic_sheet: 2

If you want to add completely new factory types beyond the basic four (Steel Mill, Refinery, Workshop, Advanced Factory), you will currently need to add them to the FactoryType.java Enum and compile the plugin. Future updates may introduce 100% custom data-driven types.
