Overview

The Marketplace System in FactoryCore is a fully-featured, GUI-based system for the player-driven economy. It allows factory owners to globally buy and sell raw materials, components, and finished products seamlessly directly from the Hub.

Selling Items

Becoming a marketplace seller is simple! Head to the Sell Items section in the Marketplace GUI to create a new offering for the server.

Creating a Listing

  1. Ensure you have the FactoryCore resources you wish to sell in your personal inventory.
  2. Click on the specific resource in your inventory while inside the Create Listing view.
  3. Pick an amount to sell.
  4. Choose your Price Strategy! You will be presented with a tier of prices mathematically generated from the resource's suggested price.
Pricing Strategy Multiplier Description
Cheap 0.5x Sell for half the suggested market rate. Perfect for rapid liquidations!
Below Market 0.75x A slightly discounted offering to undercut competitors.
Market Price 1.0x The standard suggested price configured in resources.yml.
Above Market 1.25x A premium markup for steady, high-demand items.

Browsing & Buying

Use the Browse All view to buy items listed by other players. The interface automatically updates in real-time as listings are posted, bought, or cancelled.

Finding Items

  • Page Navigation: Navigate through endless global listings using page arrows on the bottom row.
  • Resource Filter: Click the Hopper icon (Filter) at the bottom left to filter the view for highly specific resources. It will only cycle through resources that actually have active listings on the market.
  • Sorting: By default, listings are conveniently sorted by the newest additions descending. When using a resource filter, listings are sorted by price-per-unit, finding you the cheapest deals intuitively!

Making a Purchase

Clicking on any external listing opens the Confirm Purchase GUI securely indicating the unit price, total price, and your current balance. The money is automatically deposited directly into the original seller's pending earnings vault upon success.

üìã Direct Inventory Drops
Upon a successful purchase transaction, purchased items bypass standard mail systems and are instantly injected into your physical inventory! Ensure you have sufficient open slots before placing massive bulk orders!

Marketplace Mechanics

Pending Earnings & Sales Tax

Sellers do not receive their money instantly! When an item is bought, the resulting funds are placed inside the Marketplace's Pending Earnings vault. You must manually claim this vault by hitting the Collect Earnings button from the main Marketplace GUI.

Note that a flat percentage sales tax (default 5.0%) is deducted strictly from the seller's earned total to act as an economic sink.

Expiration Timers

All listings are subjected to a ticking expiration clock from the moment they are placed. If a listing is untouched by buyers after the timeout duration passes (default 168 hours / 7 days), it expires and the items are promptly returned to the seller. Similar to max active listings, investing in Business Expansion research can extend this timeout drastically for premium sellers!

Hub & GUI Overview

There are no direct player text-commands mandatory to leverage the marketplace. The system is comprehensively integrated into the FactoryCore ecosystem.

Accessing the Marketplace

  • Open standard Hub via commands (e.g. /fc hub).
  • Click on the Marketplace icon (default slot 29 in the Hub) to enter the system.
GUI Nav Button Function
Browse All (Compass) View to scroll through paginated global community listings. Includes filters.
My Listings (Book) An exclusive view of your own active listings. Clicking these will cancel them and refund the resources back to you securely.
Sell Items (Gold Ingot) Opens the listing creation handler reading purely from your personal inventory.
Collect Earnings (Gold Block) Only visible if you have pending earnings. One-click deposit for all successful unredeemed sales.

Config Reference

Marketplace settings are primarily managed inside the config.yml file under the marketplace: node! Keep your server's economy thriving by adapting these parameters!

YAML 
marketplace:
  tax-rate: 5.0                         # The percentage of gross taken from the seller on completion
  max-listings-per-player: 10           # Default cap limit for active listings per user
  listing-expiration-hours: 168         # Duration in hours before listings naturally timeout (168 = 1 week)

Developer Tips

Interact programmatically with the Marketplace System through the plugin.getMarketplaceManager() hook!

üîß Fetching & Filtering Listings
Want to generate external webboards or discord bots indicating current prices on the server? You can access the unified listings pipeline easily:

List<MarketListing> globalListings = plugin.getMarketplaceManager().getAllListings();
List<MarketListing> copperListings = plugin.getMarketplaceManager().getListingsByResource("copper_ingot");

The MarketListing object securely holds important metrics including getTotalPrice(), getListedTime(), and UUID metadata for the seller!
üîß Custom Suggestion Multipliers
By default, the MarketplaceGUI provides a hardcoded array of multiplier configurations (0.5x, 0.75x, 1.0x, 1.25x, 1.5x) calculating against the item's baseline getSellPrice(). If your server features hyper-inflation, these multipliers can be tweaked directly within MarketplaceGUI.java.
